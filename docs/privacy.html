Wayward Wander

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Wayward Wander is a scavenger hunt app that helps you explore locations through GPS-guided quests. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the app to collect only what's necessary for it to function.

Location Data

The app uses your device's GPS location to:

  • Guide you to quest destinations
  • Calculate your distance from target locations
  • Detect when you've arrived at a clue location

Your location data is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to any server.

Photos

When creating quests, you can add photos from your photo library. These photos are stored locally on your device within the app's documents folder. Photos are only shared when you explicitly export and share a quest bundle with others.

Quest Data

Quests you create or import are stored locally on your device. Quest progress is saved to your device's local storage so you can resume where you left off. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export and share a quest.

No Data Collection

Wayward Wander does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Send data to external servers
  • Display advertisements
  • Require account creation or login

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Wayward Wander does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub repository.
