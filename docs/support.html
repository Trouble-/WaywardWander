Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a quest?
Tap the "Create Quest" button on the home screen. Add a title, description, and clues with locations.

How do I share a quest?
Long-press on a quest and select "Share". This creates a .wwh file you can send to friends.

The app says I haven't arrived but I'm at the location.
GPS accuracy varies. Try the "Stuck?" button to manually confirm your arrival.

How do I import a quest?
Open a .wwh or .json quest file and choose "Open in Wayward Wander", or use the Import button on the home screen.
